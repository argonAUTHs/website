Our Tech Arsenal

KERI suite

KERI (Key Event Receipt Infrastructure) enables decentralized trust distribution through cryptographic key events, ensuring verifiable identity management without centralized ledgers or consensus algorithms. It fosters scalable, interoperable, and tamper-resistant identity systems across diverse applications.

OCA suite

Defining domain boundaries within a single system is challenging, and establishing them across systems is even more complex. OCA addresses this by enabling data-level harmonization through structured overlays, facilitating consistent, interoperable data representation.
